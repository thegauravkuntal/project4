Quality Master

"Quality Master" was created so that CMMs can be checked in a simple and easy way, even on a daily basis. The main feature of this product is that it allows measurement evaluation by surface, circular, and cylindrical measurement of three-dimensionally arranged high-precision holes.

In actual workpiece measurement, the orientation of the probe head doesn’t stay in one direction. It is measured using the four directions of probe head, enabling users to recognize attitude error as well.

TRACEABILITY

Quality Master has achieved traceability with National Standards. Inspection using our traceable, national standard-compliant gauge assists you in reducing costs.

CUSTOMERS’ UTILIZATION EXAMPLES

  • Use of CMMs in pre-start inspections.
  • Checking the accuracy of CMMs between regular and periodic calibrations.
  • Clarification of adjustment and repair details in the event of CMM problems.

SPECIFICATION

  • Made of low thermal expansion casting that is less susceptible to temperature changes.
  • Simple and stable shape with 12 precision bushings arranged in ideal positions.
  • Cylindrical shape with low concentration of stress, high resistance to temperature change, vibration, and shock.
